    Diesel/ Automotive gas oil (AGO)

    AGO is the technical name for 'diesel', a by-product of crude oil that is used to power diesel engines. Diesel engines can be found in transportation vehicles, generator sets, and heavy or light-duty machinery. Eazzyoil ensures delivery of AGO to end-users in the upstream and downstream sectors.

    Dual-purpose kerosene (Aviation & Jet Fuel

    Dual purpose kerosene (DPK); when used for household purposes it is called Household Kerosene (HHK) and when used in jet engines as aviation fuel it is called ATK which stands for Turbine Kerosene, also known as Jet A1 of aviation fuel.
